## ‚úçÔ∏è Writing Style Guidelines

### Tone & Voice:
**Professional yet Approachable**
- Use "we" and "you" for personal connection
- Avoid jargon unless explained
- Be authoritative but not condescending
- Show expertise through data and examples

**Good Example:**
‚úÖ "We understand that finding a reliable manufacturer can be challenging. That's why AIERXUAN offers transparent pricing and factory tours to build trust."

**Bad Example:**
‚ùå "Obviously, any competent buyer would know this basic information."

### Language Clarity:
**Simple > Complex**
- "use" not "utilize"
- "help" not "facilitate"
- "start" not "commence"
- "end" not "terminate"

**Active Voice > Passive Voice**
‚úÖ "AIERXUAN manufactures over 50,000 units monthly"
‚ùå "Over 50,000 units are manufactured monthly"

### Specificity:
**Vague vs Specific:**
‚ùå "Fast delivery"
‚úÖ "7-15 day delivery with 98% on-time rate"

‚ùå "High quality"
‚úÖ "ISO 9001 certified with <0.5% defect rate"

‚ùå "Competitive pricing"
‚úÖ "15-30% cost savings vs traditional OEMs"
