# AIERXUAN Company Profile

## Company Overview

**AIERXUAN** is a professional OEM/ODM manufacturer specializing in custom computing solutions for business and industrial applications. We design and manufacture high-quality laptops, mini PCs, and industrial computing equipment tailored to meet the specific needs of enterprises, system integrators, retailers, and institutions worldwide.

### Core Business Model
- **OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer)**: Manufacturing computing hardware that customers can brand and sell under their own name
- **ODM (Original Design Manufacturer)**: Providing complete product design and manufacturing services from concept to delivery
- **White Label Solutions**: Fully customizable products ready for immediate branding and deployment
