## üìã General Purchase Questions

### Q1: What should I consider when choosing a business laptop?
**A:** The key factors to consider are:
- **Purpose**: What applications and tasks will you run?
- **Portability**: Will you travel frequently? (Weight under 1.6kg is ideal)
- **Performance**: Processor type, RAM capacity, and storage speed
- **Display**: Screen size, brightness (400+ nits recommended), and resolution
- **Battery Life**: At least 8 hours for all-day productivity
- **Security**: TPM 2.0, fingerprint reader, and physical security lock
- **Budget**: Balance between features and cost

**Source**: [Laptop Guide: 10 Questions to Ask Yourself Before Buying](https://news.techdefenders.com/blog/laptop-guide-10-questions-to-ask-yourself-before-buying)

### Q2: How long will my laptop last?
**A:** A well-maintained business laptop typically lasts:
- **3-5 years** for daily business use
- **5-7 years** with lighter usage
- **Factors affecting longevity**: Build quality, component quality, usage intensity, and proper maintenance
- **Tip**: Investing in higher-quality components (like SSD storage and adequate RAM) extends useful life

**Source**: [10 Questions to Ask Yourself Before Buying a Laptop](https://thekeeblog.com/10-questions-to-ask-yourself-before-buying-a-laptop/)

### Q3: Desktop or Laptop - which is better for business?
**A:** Choose a **laptop** if you:
- Need to work from multiple locations
- Travel for business
- Attend meetings with presentations
- Want flexibility in workspace

Choose a **desktop** if you:
- Work from a fixed location
- Need maximum performance at lower cost
- Require multiple large monitors

**Source**: [Computer Guide: 4 Questions to Ask Before You Buy](https://www.marconet.com/blog/computer-guide-4-questions-to-ask-before-your-buy)

### Q6: How much RAM do I need?
**A:** RAM recommendations:

| **6-8GB** | Basic office work | Web browsing, email, light Office work |
| **12-16GB** | Standard business | Multitasking, video calls, data analysis |

**All our products support up to 32GB RAM** for future-proofing your investment.

### Q7: What's the difference between DDR4 and LPDDR4?
**A:**
- **DDR4**: Standard RAM, usually upgradable via SODIMM slots
- **LPDDR4**: Low-power variant, soldered (not upgradable), better battery life

**Our products:**
- **Upgradable (DDR4)**: DU61, Air15-Ultra, Air14-Pro2025 (can expand to 32GB later)
- **Soldered (LPDDR4)**: Air14, Air15, Yaoxing 15S (choose capacity at purchase)

## üîã Battery & Power Questions

### Q14: How long should the battery last?
**A:** Business laptop battery expectations:
- **Minimum**: 6-8 hours for all-day work
- **Recommended**: 8-10 hours for meetings without charging
- **Our products**: 3-10 hours depending on usage and model

**Battery life factors:**
- Screen brightness (biggest factor)
- Running applications (video calls drain faster)
- Processor efficiency (newer processors are more efficient)
- Battery capacity (measured in Wh)

**Source**: [6 Questions to Ask Before Buying a New Laptop](https://www.makeuseof.com/questions-to-ask-before-buying-new-laptop/)

### Q15: What is battery capacity (Wh)?
**A:** Watt-hours (Wh) measure total battery energy:
- **38Wh**: Entry-level, 3-5 hours (Air14, Air15, Air15-Ultra)
- **50-60Wh**: Mid-range, 6-8 hours (DU61: 57.75Wh)
- **70Wh+**: Premium, 10+ hours

**Higher capacity = Longer runtime** but may add weight.
